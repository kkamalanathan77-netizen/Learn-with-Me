Class 8 Science Questions

1. Crop grown in the summer season is called:
a) Rabi
b) Kharif
c) Zaid
d) Cereal
Correct Answer: Zaid
2. Which nutrient helps in growth and repair of body?
a) Carbohydrates
b) Fats
c) Proteins
d) Vitamins
Correct Answer: Proteins
3. Synthetic fibres are also called:
a) Natural fibres
b) Artificial fibres
c) Plant fibres
d) Animal fibres
Correct Answer: Artificial fibres
4. Which plastic can be recycled?
a) Thermosetting plastic
b) Bakelite
c) Melamine
d) Thermoplastic
Correct Answer: Thermoplastic
5. Coal, petroleum and natural gas are called:
a) Renewable resources
b) Fossil fuels
c) Minerals
d) Metals
Correct Answer: Fossil fuels
