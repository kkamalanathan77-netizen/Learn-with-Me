NCERT Questions & Answers

Q1. What is agriculture?

Answer: Agriculture is the practice of growing crops and rearing animals for food and other products.

Q2. Name the steps in crop production.

Answer: Preparation of soil, sowing seeds, adding manure/fertilizers, irrigation, harvesting, storage.

Q3. Why is crop rotation important?

Answer: Crop rotation helps maintain soil fertility and reduces pests and diseases.
