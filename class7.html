NCERT Questions & Answers

Q1. What is nutrition?

Answer: Nutrition is the process of taking in food, its digestion and absorption to provide energy and growth.

Q2. Name the types of nutrition in animals.

Answer: Animals show three types of nutrition: herbivorous, carnivorous, and omnivorous.

Q3. What is herbivorous nutrition?

Answer: Herbivorous animals eat only plants, e.g., cow, rabbit.
